Getting to know Django

What is Django?

Django is a Python Web framework that does alot of work for you when creating your web applications. They use a nice sentence to describe their goal and it goas like this - You can focus on writing your app without needing to reinvent the wheel.

Django provides couple of layers that help you organise your work:

  • The model layer - For structuring and manipulating the data of your Web application.
  • The view layer - Takes care of the logic responsible for processing a user's request and for returning the response.
  • The template layer - Provides a designer-friendly syntax for rendering the information to be presented to the user.
  • Forms - Framework to facilitate the creation of form and the manipulation of form data.

On top of this, there are lots of settings and extra manipulations that you can do, whilst working on your project.
